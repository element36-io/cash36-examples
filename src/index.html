Wire-transfer Euro or CHF to Aragon Smart Contracts

All transaction are handled within KYC-ed environment which is enforced by smart contracts (ERC20 stablecoins).

  1. Do payment
  2. Onboard & KYC yourself if not done yet
  3. Funds will be sent to Aragon Smart Contract

Aragon with Testnetwork

Simulated payments & KYC on rinkeby network.

You may log into demo-server with our demo-user: demo@element36.io and password: Element36.io

Check out incoming funds at the Aragon contract.

Aragon on Mainnet with real money

Aragon contract on mainnet using real (live) payments and KYC.

After the payment you need to do full KYC on cash36.io. If done, your payment will converted to our stablecoin and sent to the contract.

Check out the Aragon contract on Aragon.org.

You may create your own Aragon Contracts and clone our github project to create your own UI. Contact us if you want to create your own Stablecoins.
