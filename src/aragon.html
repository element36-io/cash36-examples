Send Euro or CHF directly to Aragon Smart Contracts with element36

All transaction are handled within KYC-ed environment which is enforced by an ERC-20 Contract for a Stablecoin.

  1. Do payment
  2. KYC yourself not done yet
  3. Funds will be sent to Aragon Smart Contract

Aragon with Testnetwork

Similated payments, KYC and rinkeby network.

Do KYC on demo-server or github-demo-server, check out the Aragon contract on Aragon.org.

Aragon on Mainnet with real currencies

Aragon contract on mainnet using real (live) payments and KYC.

After (a small) payment you need to do full KYC on cash36.io. If done, your payment will converted to our stablecoin and sent to the contract. check out the Aragon contract on Aragon.org.

You may create your own Aragon Contracts and clone our github project to create your own UI. Contact us if you want to create your own Stablecoins.
